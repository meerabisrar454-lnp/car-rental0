Top Car Rental Questions

How does it work?
Our car rental process is simple. Just select your dates, choose a car, provide your details, and book. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.
Can I rent a car without a credit card?
Yes, we accept debit cards and cash deposits for certain vehicle categories. However, a credit card is required for premium vehicles and for security purposes.
What's included in the rental price?
Our rental prices include basic insurance, unlimited mileage, and 24/7 roadside assistance. Additional services like GPS, child seats, and additional drivers are available for an extra fee.
Can I modify or cancel my reservation?
Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your pickup time without any penalty. Cancellations made less than 24 hours before may incur a fee.
What do I need to rent a car?
You'll need a valid driver's license held for at least one year, a credit card in the driver's name, and a form of identification (passport or ID card). International visitors may need an International Driving Permit.
